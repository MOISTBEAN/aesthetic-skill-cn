# Finance Terminal Preview

## Representative screen

**Scenario:** A self-directed Hong Kong stock investor reviews portfolio discipline before adding risk.

The header states `港股交易中 · 报价延迟 15 分钟 · HKD · 15:45`. A compact account summary sits above the positions table, while a risk strip highlights:

- `1 项卖出条件已触发`
- `单一持仓 12.4%，高于自设上限 10%`
- `2 个持有逻辑超过 30 天未复核`

Selecting a watchlist row opens a research panel with thesis status, buy reason, sell conditions, valuation range, catalysts, risk evidence, and linked transactions.

## Required negative preview

Show at least one state from each group:

- performance: `当日浮动盈亏 -HK$1,284（未含费用）`
- thesis: `逻辑减弱`
- discipline: `卖出条件已触发`
- market data: `停牌` or `报价不可用`
- operations: `导入完成 48 / 52 笔`

## Visual notes

Use disciplined charcoal surfaces, compact aligned rows, tabular numerals, one research-blue accent, and semantic market/risk colors with textual cues. Avoid coins, neon, ticker-wall theater, and any visual imitation of a real terminal.
