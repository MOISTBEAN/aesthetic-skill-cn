# Rejected: Generic Chinese SaaS Page

## Candidate

A blue-purple hero says `AI 驱动的一站式平台，重新定义团队效率`. Below it are six rounded feature cards: `智能洞察`, `高效协同`, `安全可靠`, `无限扩展`, `增长引擎`, and `极致体验`. The product preview is a decorative browser frame with a fake chart and no usable workflow.

## Why it feels AI-generated

- The headline says nothing about the user, object, or decision.
- The six cards can describe any software product.
- The preview has no input, output, exception, or next action.
- Visual identity depends on standard AI gradients and floating cards.
- The page type was invented from “make a SaaS page” instead of selected from a contract.

## Violated gates

- Product Swap Test
- Fake SaaS Copy Test
- Card Grid Slop Test
- Decorative Preview Test
- Visual Tell Test
- Output Contract selection

## Better structure

Do not restyle the same page. Select a contract first. If the product reviews AI output, use `AI Review / Human Approval Workflow` with `Review Queue Layout`, locked evidence and approval modules, a failed or pending state, and explicit human actions.
