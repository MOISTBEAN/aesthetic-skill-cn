# Golden Case: Xiaohongshu Creator Workbench

## Selected contract

`Xiaohongshu Creator Workbench` for a three-person home-content team planning one week of posts and brand deliverables.

## Selected skeleton

`Calendar + Detail Layout`.

1. account and status filters
2. weekly content calendar
3. selected-day content list
4. mobile preview
5. revision queue

## Allowed modules

- 内容日历
- 选题池
- 封面状态
- 标题 A/B 测试
- 笔记状态
- 品牌合作 CRM
- 待修改项
- 移动端预览

No engagement overview, AI recommendation, or generic growth module is allowed.

## User actions

- 安排周三 19:30 发布
- 标记封面字号待改
- 修改超过 20 字的标题
- 提交品牌确认
- 记录高收藏笔记的可复用结构

## Negative states

- 待改封面
- 标题需重写
- 品牌待确认
- 周三发布时间冲突
- 收藏率低于近 7 篇均值

Each state names an owner and next step.

## Why it passes Product Swap Test

The content calendar, cover review, title variants, publishing rhythm, mobile note preview, and brand approval cannot become a finance or contract product by changing the name. Structure follows creator operations rather than a generic dashboard.
